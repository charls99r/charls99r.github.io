About Me

I am a product-focused engineer with hands-on experience designing, building, and validating embedded and automotive systems. My engineering background is largely self-taught, developed through practical work, experimentation, and real-world problem solving. My work spans the full development lifecycle — from concept and PCB design to firmware development, system integration, and on-vehicle testing.

I enjoy working at the intersection of hardware and software, especially on systems that end up in real vehicles or physical products. I have worked on standalone ECUs, vehicle electronics, and IoT devices, taking ideas from prototype to deployment.

I value clean design, reliability, and practical engineering solutions. I am comfortable owning projects end-to-end and collaborating across disciplines, and I am always willing to learn new technologies or domains when needed. I adapt quickly and enjoy picking up unfamiliar systems to deliver working, real-world results.
